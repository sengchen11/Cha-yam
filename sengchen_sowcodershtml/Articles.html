Mental Health care in Bhutan


The government of Bhutan continues to help fund on the Mental Health care of it's people and financing is mainly oriented towards training of health workers and providing essential drugs. Treatment, including the supply of psychotropic drugs is provided free by the government.The government along with the support and help from UNICEF and other organisations are raising the awareness on the mental health to the people of Bhutan.
